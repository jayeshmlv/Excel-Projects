# Style Advisor Performance Evaluation

## Overview

This project analyzes the performance of Style Advisors using customer evaluation data collected across different regions. The objective was to evaluate employee performance, identify regional trends, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer service quality.

---

## Business Objective

- Evaluate Style Advisor performance using customer evaluation scores.
- Categorize employees into performance groups.
- Compare regional performance.
- Identify strengths and improvement areas.
- Support management with data-driven recommendations.

---

## Key Analysis

- Performance grading based on evaluation scores.
- Zone-wise average performance comparison.
- High Performer percentage by zone.
- Evaluation score trends over time.
- Evaluation criteria analysis.
- High vs Low Performer comparison.

---

## Key Insights

- North Zone recorded the highest average evaluation score.
- East Zone had the highest percentage of High Performers.
- South Zone consistently showed the lowest performance.
- Store Ambiance and First Impression received the highest scores.
- Closure & Care and Recommendations were the weakest evaluation criteria.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Conduct targeted training programs for South Zone employees.
- Improve customer closing and recommendation techniques.
- Replicate best practices followed by top-performing regions.
- Implement monthly performance monitoring.
- Introduce reward and recognition programs for high performers.
